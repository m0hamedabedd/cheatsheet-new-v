Generate Cheat Sheets from Lecture Notes

Upload your lecture notes in .txt, .md, or .pdf format and get a concise, one-page HTML cheat sheet.

Welcome to Smart Cheat Sheet Generator

Your digital assistant for turning long lectures into concise, structured, and visually clear cheat sheets.

  • ✅ Paste lecture or textbook content
  • ✅ Automatically extract and organize the key points
  • ✅ Render clean, one-page cheat sheets with formulas, definitions, and visuals
  • ✅ Support LaTeX-style equations using MathJax
  • ✅ Export or print your notes instantly

Built for simplicity and clarity, this tool helps you focus on what matters most — understanding and reviewing, not rewriting.

🔍 Who is it for?

  • 📚 University & high school students
  • 👨‍🏫 Teachers preparing summaries
  • 🤖 Learners of technical fields like math, physics, and engineering

🛠 How it works

  1. Paste or write your lecture notes.
  2. Choose your cheat sheet style and theme.
  3. Click "Generate Cheat Sheet" and get instant results.
  4. Export as HTML or PDF — or just print and study!

💡 Why use this tool?

  • Saves time on making notes
  • Highlights only the essential information
  • Supports visual learning with icons, diagrams, and math formatting
